Stay Safe Online

? Prevention Tips
?? Use strong, unique passwords for every account (combine letters, numbers, symbols) 
?? Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps 
?? Do not click on suspicious links received via SMS, email, or WhatsApp 
?? Verify unknown calls/messages claiming to be from banks, colleges, or companies 
?? Keep your apps and devices updated to avoid security vulnerabilities 
?? Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like banking or logging into important accounts

?? Financial / UPI Safety Tips (Important)
?? Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank 
?? Always check the receiver’s name before sending money via UPI to avoid wrong transfers 
?? Do not approve “collect requests” from unknown people in apps like PhonePe, Google Pay, or Paytm 
?? Install apps only from official sources like Google Play Store or Apple App Store

?? Report Cyber Crimes Immediately
?? National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in 
?? Helpline Number: 1930
