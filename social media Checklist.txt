Checklist for responsible social media use as a college student

✅Do's
✅Think before you post or share content 
✅Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication 
✅Verify information before sharing (avoid spreading fake news) 
✅Maintain a positive and respectful tone online 
✅Adjust privacy settings to protect your personal information

❌Dont's
❌Do not share personal details like address, phone number, or OTP 
❌Avoid posting offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content 
❌Do not accept friend requests from unknown people 
❌Avoid spending excessive time on social media 
❌Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
